# CoachFlux — AI Coaching App for Simon @BetterCreating
## RevenueCat Shipyard Hackathon ($20,000 Prize)

## Brief: Simon @BetterCreating
> "Create a clean, minimal mobile app where users can browse, create, and share AI coaches, add personal context/values, and start chatting immediately. No complex setup, just guidance at the right moment."

## App Concept
**CoachFlux** — Your AI coaching studio. Browse community coaches, create your own, share with friends. Gemini Flash 2.0 powered. Minimal, beautiful, instant.

## Core Features (MVP)
1. **Browse Coaches** — Curated library of AI coach personas (Productivity, Mindset, Health, Career, Creative, Finance)
2. **Create Coach** — Custom AI coach builder: name, persona, expertise, tone, avatar
3. **Share Coaches** — Share your coach via link/QR, community marketplace
4. **Chat Interface** — Clean chat with your chosen coach, powered by Gemini Flash 2.0
5. **Personal Context** — Add values, goals, challenges → coach adapts to YOU
6. **Coach Sessions** — Scheduled micro-sessions (5-min daily check-ins)
7. **Progress Tracking** — Insights from conversations, streak tracking

## Monetization (RevenueCat)
- **Free Tier:** 3 coaches, 10 messages/day, 1 custom coach
- **Pro ($12.99/mo):** Unlimited coaches, unlimited messages, unlimited custom coaches, session history, export
- **Coach Tier ($99/mo):** For coaches/creators — publish to marketplace, analytics, client management

## Tech Stack
- **Framework:** Flutter (iOS + Android)
- **AI:** Google Gemini Flash 2.0 API
- **Backend:** Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Functions)
- **Payments:** RevenueCat SDK
- **Design:** MindFlow-inspired zen aesthetic (Night Sky + Lavender + Sage)
